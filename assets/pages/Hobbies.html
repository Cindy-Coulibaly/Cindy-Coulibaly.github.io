Hobbies

Art

I have several hobbies, but one of my main ones is painting, drawing, or engaging in any artsy activities. I haven't had much time during this past year to actually paint and draw, but I still have some unfinished projects and other completed ones. Here are some of them

Letter C

This structure was crafted from recycled cereal boxes, cut into the shape of the first letter of my name. I then painted it with acrylic paint and applied a coat of paint varnish to finish it.

The Simpson

This project was relatively straightforward as it involved recreating something that already existed. I painted it using acrylics during a moment of boredom. Although I don't watch The Simpsons much, I just felt like expressing myself through painting.

Face parts

This was primarily done for practice since it had been a while since I attempted facial features. However, I didn't want to fully commit to drawing an entire face, so I focused on sketching the mouth, eyes, and nose in different spots. It was more about drawing whatever I felt in the moment.

Meliodas

This painting depicts the main character from an anime I used to watch called 'Seven Deadly Sins.' I was particularly fond of a specific moment in the anime and decided to capture that scene through my painting.

Work In Progress

I began working on this painting in 2021 but never completed it. Nevertheless, I find myself revisiting it from time to time, gradually making progress. It's a collage painting featuring album covers of music that I truly enjoy.
